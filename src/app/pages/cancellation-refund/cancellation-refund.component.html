Cancellations, Refunds & Trial Policy

PathoPro is committed to building long-term and trustworthy relationships with its customers.

Once a product or service is purchased and the payment is successfully processed, the software license is activated immediately. After activation, no refunds will be issued, as software licenses cannot be returned, revoked, or transferred.

To help customers evaluate our solutions, PathoPro offers a 6-month trial period. During this trial, customers can fully explore the product features. Our support team is available to assist with installation and configuration via email or telephone. Customers are advised to thoroughly evaluate the product before making any purchase decision.

Trial Period & Data Export Policy

At the end of the 6-month trial period, access to data export functionality will be restricted. Customers will not be allowed to export their data unless they subscribe to a paid plan.

To enable data export, customers must subscribe to at least a one-month paid plan. Once the subscription is active, permission to export data will be granted according to the selected plan.

Cancellation Policy

  • No cancellations are permitted for purchases made under promotional offers, discounts, festive sales, or limited-time deals.
  • All purchases are non-refundable and non-transferable once completed.
  • Customers are responsible for evaluating the product’s suitability during the trial period prior to purchase.

Third-Party Service Disclaimer

PathoPro shall not be held responsible for service interruptions or failures caused by third-party providers such as hosting services, servers, content providers, or external platforms.

Any coordination or liaison support provided by PathoPro with third-party vendors will be chargeable as per the company’s agreed rates.

Refund Policy

PathoPro does not offer a money-back policy for any product or service. However, customers may notify our support team within 12 hours of receiving the product or service if they are not satisfied.

Due to the nature of digital products and services, refunds are not guaranteed upon cancellation. For subscription-based services, payment for the next billing cycle will be processed only after reviewing the current month’s service delivery.

PathoPro does not guarantee specific outcomes related to performance metrics such as traffic, rankings, or usage results and shall not be held responsible for refund claims based on such expectations.
